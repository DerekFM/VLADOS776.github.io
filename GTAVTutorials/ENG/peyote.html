Peyote Location

Introduction

In order, to dispel all doubts, Peyote (Lophophora) - really existing plant, belonging to the genus of the plant family Cactaceae. These unusual plants first met in versions for PS4 and XONE. And gamers was noticed, that after the use of peyote your hero began of hallucinating, that moves his consciousness in "skin" of a random animals!

Features

Features of the use of Peyote:

  • Hallucination interrupted in two cases - if the animal dies or you was canceled hallucinogenic effects of the plant.
  • Animal fully controlled and has its own characteristics. For example, you can make a sound of "animal" by pressing automotive signal; earning stars investigation. Gangsters or police may shoot and kill you in the end. But you can to attack enemies - butting, bite or defecate!
  • After collecting all 27 peyotes, they again be "grow up" in the same places. Therefore, the number of transformations are endless! And yes, after collect their full collection you will not get achievements or progress :(
