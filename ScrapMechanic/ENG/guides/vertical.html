A guide to Vertical Climbing!

This guide will show you how to create a very simple and basic vehicle, which can do some awesome wallclimbing! I've played around with it for a while, it defies Gravity, way more reliable than you might expect it to! This little device is absolutely amazing!

Step 1

Make a basic foundation for the build.

-Place an 8x2 rectangle made out of metal ontop of the lift.

-Place a driver s seat in the middle, and 2 engines on the remaining sides,

Step 2

-Place a Pipe Corner next to the engine facing upwards.

-Place a Pipe Long ontop of te Pipe Corner.

-Repeat this on all 4 sides.
Step 3

-Place a Pipe Join on top of the Pipe Long

-Make sure the Pipe Join is facing up and outwards.

-Repeat this on all 4 sides.
Step 4

-Place a Pipe Corner facing down on the Pipe Join.

-Repeat this on all 4 sides.

-Place a Bearing on the front 2 Pipe Corners. This will become your steering.
Step 5

-Place a Pipe Short on the Pipe Corners.

-Repeat this on all 4 sides.

-Place a regular Suspension on the Pipe Short.

-Repeat this on all 4 sides.

Step 6

-Place a Pipe Corner facing outwards underneath the Suspension.

-Place a Bearing on the Pipe Corner.

-Repeat this on all 4 sides.

Step 7

-Place Big Wheels on the Bearings.

-Repeat this on all sides.
Step 8

-Place a total of 4 Thrusters on top of the vehicle.

-Place 2 Thrusters on the back on the engine
Step 9

-Place a Button on both sides of the vehicle.

MAKE SURE IT'S ON BOTH SIDES!
Step 10

-Get your connector tool and connect Bearings of the wheels to the engines.

-Connect the Bearings of the front steering to the driver s seat.

-Connect the engines to the driver s seat.
Step 11

-Connect 1 button to all 4 upwards Thrusters.
Step 12

-Connect the other button to both of the Backwards Thrusters.
Step 13

-Connect both of the buttons to the driver s seat.
Step 14

-Put both engine speeds to the middle.
You're all done!

You can now enjoy your vehicle!

Assuming you followed the tutorial perfectly, this is how you have to drive:

-Press 1 to make your vehicle stick to the surface it's on.

-Press 2 when it becomes hard to thrust up. Usually the wheels don't have enough power to drive vertically, so the jets at the back solve that.

It takes a little getting used to, but it can climb most objects in the map!
